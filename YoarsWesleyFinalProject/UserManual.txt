1:Run the program.
2:When Windows pop up, choose the calculator you want to use.
3:If you choose the Simple Calculator, you click a number.
	3a:Choose an operator(add, subtract, multiply, or divide).
	3b:Then click another number.
	3c:Lastly, hit the equals symbol(=).
4:If you choose the Advanced Calculator, you click a number.
	4a: Then you choose to find that numbers percent, square root, or square.
5:For both calculators, in order to clear the text, you must hit clear if you want to start over.
6:When you are done using the calculator, please close out of both of the windows to quit the program.
